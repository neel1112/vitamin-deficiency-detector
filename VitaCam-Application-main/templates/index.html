About Our Analysis

How It Works

Our AI system analyzes facial features to detect potential vitamin deficiencies:

  • Advanced facial feature analysis
  • Pattern recognition of deficiency indicators
  • Cross-referencing with medical databases
  • Probability-based deficiency assessment

What We Detect

Our system can identify potential deficiencies in:

  • Vitamin D - through skin tone and texture analysis
  • Vitamin B12 - through lip and tongue examination
  • Iron - through conjunctiva and nail bed analysis
  • Vitamin C - through gum and skin health assessment
  • Vitamin A - through eye and skin condition evaluation

Important Notes

Please remember:

  • This is a screening tool, not a diagnostic test
  • Results should be discussed with healthcare providers
  • Regular medical check-ups are still essential
  • Results may vary based on image quality
  • System accuracy improves with better quality images
