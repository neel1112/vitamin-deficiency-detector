DISEASES

Problems that affect the body

FATIGUE

Fatigue symptoms refer to feelings of extreme tiredness, lack of energy, or exhaustion that can be physical, mental, or both.

×

FATIGUE - Detailed Information

Fatigue is a common symptom that can be caused by various factors:

  • Physical causes: Lack of sleep, poor diet, dehydration, anemia
  • Mental causes: Stress, anxiety, depression
  • Medical conditions: Thyroid problems, diabetes, heart disease
  • Lifestyle factors: Sedentary lifestyle, excessive caffeine, alcohol use

Common symptoms include:

  • Persistent tiredness
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Muscle weakness
  • Headaches
  • Irritability

Treatment options:

  • Improve sleep habits
  • Regular exercise
  • Balanced diet
  • Stress management
  • Medical consultation if persistent

NAUSEA

Nausea is the uneasy or uncomfortable sensation in the stomach that often makes you feel like you need to vomit.

NAUSEA - Detailed Information

Nausea can be caused by various factors:

  • Gastrointestinal issues: Food poisoning, stomach flu
  • Motion sickness
  • Pregnancy (morning sickness)
  • Medications and treatments
  • Migraines

Common symptoms include:

  • Queasy feeling in stomach
  • Increased saliva production
  • Sweating
  • Loss of appetite

Treatment options:

  • Rest and hydration
  • Ginger tea or supplements
  • Small, bland meals
  • Anti-nausea medications
  • Acupressure wristbands

WEIGHT LOSS

Weight loss refers to a decrease in body weight, which can result from a variety of factors.

WEIGHT LOSS - Detailed Information

Weight loss can be intentional or unintentional:

  • Intentional causes: Diet, exercise, lifestyle changes
  • Unintentional causes: Medical conditions, stress, medications
  • Metabolic disorders
  • Digestive problems

Common symptoms include:

  • Decreased appetite
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle weakness
  • Changes in bowel habits

Treatment options:

  • Balanced diet plan
  • Regular exercise
  • Medical evaluation
  • Nutritional supplements
  • Stress management

SKIN

Skin deficiency refers to a lack of essential nutrients, hydration, or care, leading to various skin problems.

SKIN DEFICIENCY - Detailed Information

Common skin deficiencies and their causes:

  • Vitamin A deficiency: Dry, rough skin
  • Vitamin C deficiency: Poor wound healing
  • Vitamin E deficiency: Skin aging
  • Zinc deficiency: Acne, rashes
  • Essential fatty acids: Dry, flaky skin

Common symptoms include:

  • Dryness and flakiness
  • Slow wound healing
  • Premature aging
  • Acne and breakouts
  • Skin discoloration

Treatment options:

  • Balanced diet rich in vitamins
  • Proper hydration
  • Regular skincare routine
  • Sun protection
  • Nutritional supplements

HAIR LOSS

Hair loss, also called alopecia, is when you lose more hair than usual, leading to thinning or bald spots.

HAIR LOSS - Detailed Information

Common causes of hair loss:

  • Genetic factors (androgenetic alopecia)
  • Hormonal changes
  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Stress and anxiety
  • Medical conditions

Common symptoms include:

  • Gradual thinning on top of head
  • Circular or patchy bald spots
  • Sudden loosening of hair
  • Full-body hair loss
  • Patches of scaling

Treatment options:

  • Medications (minoxidil, finasteride)
  • Hair transplant surgery
  • Laser therapy
  • Nutritional supplements
  • Stress management

PALE SKIN

Having pale or yellowish skin can be a sign of different health conditions.

PALE SKIN - Detailed Information

Common causes of pale skin:

  • Anemia (iron deficiency)
  • Vitamin B12 deficiency
  • Poor circulation
  • Low blood pressure
  • Liver problems

Common symptoms include:

  • Lighter than normal skin tone
  • Yellowish tint (jaundice)
  • Fatigue
  • Dizziness
  • Shortness of breath

Treatment options:

  • Iron supplements
  • Vitamin B12 injections
  • Dietary changes
  • Regular exercise
  • Medical evaluation

WEAK SYSTEM

Weak immune system is has sick often, especially with colds, flu, or infections.

WEAK IMMUNE SYSTEM - Detailed Information

Common causes of weak immune system:

  • Chronic stress
  • Poor nutrition
  • Lack of sleep
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Underlying medical conditions

Common symptoms include:

  • Frequent infections
  • Slow wound healing
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Digestive problems
  • Autoimmune disorders

Treatment options:

  • Balanced diet rich in vitamins
  • Regular exercise
  • Adequate sleep
  • Stress management
  • Probiotics and supplements

VISION PROBLEM

Vision problems could be due to various factors, including eye strain, nutritional deficiencies, aging, or underlying medical conditions.

VISION PROBLEMS - Detailed Information

Common causes of vision problems:

  • Refractive errors (nearsightedness, farsightedness)
  • Age-related changes
  • Vitamin A deficiency
  • Eye strain
  • Underlying health conditions

Common symptoms include:

  • Blurred vision
  • Double vision
  • Eye strain
  • Headaches
  • Difficulty seeing at night

Treatment options:

  • Regular eye exams
  • Corrective lenses
  • Eye exercises
  • Nutritional supplements
  • Proper lighting and screen time management
